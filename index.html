Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Fueling economic growth through cutting-edge technology and digital transformation.

Sustainability

Championing renewable energy, environmental stewardship, and long-term climate resilience.

Infrastructure

Revolutionizing connectivity and urban development for economic and social progress.

Empowerment

Elevating youth, women, and marginalized communities to drive inclusive development.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh must rise to the challenge of becoming Bangladesh 2.0. We cannot simply go back to the old system that held us back. This is a unique opportunity to redefine our future, creating a country built on innovation, inclusion, and sustainability. Together, we must shape a nation that eliminates poverty, empowers youth, and leads by example globally."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
